Animal Sanctuary

Guests have the opportunity to visit the animal sanctuary, which involves both an enclosure as well as a tubed aquarium. Guests can get up and close to many land and water based animals in a safe and controlled manner. These animals includes lions, pandas, cheetahs, spider monkeys and many more. Whilst our 360 degree aquarium plays host to manta rays, tiger sharks, killer whales and many more intersting water species.
