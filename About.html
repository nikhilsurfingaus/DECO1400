Team Excitement Island Resorts have put together some FAQ’s to help plan your stay with us. If you have any questions please give us a call on 07 5514 4400 or email excitementresort@gmail.com The whole team are ready and waiting to welcome you to Excitement Island Resort.



FAQ

Check-in and Check out process?
  • Check-in is from 2pm for family room, 12pm for couples sites & 11 am for all other rooms
  • Check-out is no later than 10 am for all rooms
  • Our office closes at 6 pm Saturday to Thursday & 7 pm on Friday
Planning your stay with us?
  • ID Wristbands: must always be worn during your stay. If you happen to break one – please return it to reception for a replacement
  • GATE ACCESS: Only one gate code per car can be used. Due to limited space, we only allow 1 car per room. Extra vehicles must be parked outside the resort
  • POOL & WATERPARK RULES: These rules are posted around the pool and waterpark and are for the safety and comfort of all our guests
  • QUIET TIME: We have a lockout rule for all nighclubs and bars of 3am, we prefer guests keep noise to a minimum after 3am.
Is there Internet or WiFi access available?
  • WiFi is available on the enitre island
  • For NBN wifi for our business guests NBN WiFi is available, surcharge applies
Q: How does room service work?
  • All rooms are self contained with a FREE service clean for stays of 7 days or longer
  • Our room service for food is 24/7 with chefs from around the world on hand, take a look at your menu in your room for dishes to order
