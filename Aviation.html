Activities > Aviation

Plane Spotting

The aviation spotting activity involves various camera and videoing opportunities surrouding the islands main international airport. Aircraft the airport can handle include the A320, A330, A350, A380, 747, 777, 767, 757, 737, 717, F100, F70, E190, E170, DHC-800, DHC-400, ATR-72, ATR-42, SAAB340 as well as Aircraft from both business jets and general aviation. You don't want to miss out on this possibility to capture many stunning photos.


Sky Diving

Sky diving is a thrilling sport, well here on Excitement Island expereince either solo or tandom jumps from our C152 aircraft. At a drop altitude of 10000ft with the wind gushing blissfully you'll have a view of our island that no other person can expereince. With a fall time of 84 seconds our fully trained and certified staff will ensure that you reach the ground safely whilst similtaneously experiencing a unique perspective of the island.


Fighter Jet Expereience

Our newest aviation activity makes use of retired RAAF fighter jet aircraft. Guests will be able expereince a 30 minute thrill in either an F18 Hornet or F35 Lightning sitting behind a fulll trained and qualified RAAF fighter pilot. Reaching speeds of up to 850km/h with g-forces maxing out at 16G, this will be ride you never forget. Your full trained pilot will also take you through manoeuvres such as barrel rolls, spins, high altitude stalls and low altitude ground passes.
