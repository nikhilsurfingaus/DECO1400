Activities > Relax

Pool

With beautiful, tropical landscaping, the Islands Pool is a great spot to relax, unwind and enjoy our Island’s lovely, mild weather. Whether you are parents looking for to to unwind bring the kids down to the pools and sit back and relx knowing our trained lifeguards ensure saftey around the pool. As for our adult guests grab an alcoholic drink from our water based bar.



Island Cruise

experience the islands beachs in air-conditioned comfort, on board a Cruise. See for yourself what makes this beach such a pure and pristine natural wonder. If you’re feeling particularly energetic, you can take a walk to the lighthouse Lookout, which offers some of the best views out over the islands. With up to 17 different dining experiences to choose we're plating up everything from casual to connoisseur, so you how you dine.
