Activities > Sport

Golf

the Island plays host to an 18 hole round gold game. Based on a separate smaller island from the main island, guests take an amazing helicopter ride across the reef littered gorge separating the two islands on their way to a gorgeous golf circuit. Each guest is provided with a golf kart with clubs complementary with the booking. Guests can also book a member of staff to drive them as well as write down scores for each hole.


Football

Soccer teams of all ages all play together based on skill level, allowing for the more competitive players to play together and less competitive players together ensuring all guests have fun. This unique sporting activity offers views of the mountain tops and vast ocean surrounding the island.
