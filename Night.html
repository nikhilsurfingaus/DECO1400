Nightclubs

The Hottest nightclubs on the Island! Experience clubbing at its best in breathtaking surroundings and with a true Vegas atmosphere right here on Excitement Island. With premium drink offerings, a delicious cocktail menus and world class DJ’s and entertainment you won't be dissapointed. Check out the night clubbing scene here on the Island with a total of 12 clubs running every night. Our venues offers exclusive areas with custom lounge seating, personal hostesses and VIP Booths for all patrons.



Bars

If sipping a cocktail on an unspoilt tropical island sounds like your idea of a good time, you’re in the right place. Excitment Island's Bars are situated right on the beachfront at the Marina. So, once you’ve explored the sheltered waters and fringing reef, dry off and admire the ocean views with a glass of something sweet in your hand.




Volanic Eruption Tours

Witnessing a volcanic eruption is one of the most awe-inspiring displays in nature. On our tours, we try to see lava and ash from close and safe distance. "Big Volcy" Volcano remains active, spouting orange lava, venting steam, glowing, and sputtering. When conditions are safe, it’s possible to walk around the volcano's edge on the 14km Crater trail.
