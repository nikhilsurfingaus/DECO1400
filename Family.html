Activities > Family

Kids Club

As this island is welcoming to guests from all ages, if you are a parent or caregiver our kids club can keep your kids entertained for hours on end. Your kids will engage in acitivities such as puzzles, games, cooking, playing, building. We provide all food which is healthy and meets all your kids dietry needs. Head over to booking and select kids club if you would like to book your child into this awesome expereince.


Mini Golf

Need time off with the kids, mini golf is your answer. With a 25 hole game, you and your kids will enjoy this tropical course which has many tricks and hidden gems in conquering each hole. Work togther as a Family with your kids to finish the course. We garentee you wont be dissapointed.


Trail Walks

Excitement Island is one of the word finest in terms of its trail walks. Spend a day with your family walking the island, looking at the volcanos, mountains, jungle tree tops, river, pristine lake and finally the cliff edges. The trail totals 15km, and can either be done on foot if you are really game. Or the option exists to hire a golf cart to have the same experience.


Go Karts

Go karting has both karts available for parent-child tandon, or single riders with larger more powerful karts. The circuit is a replica of the Formula 1 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix track, to give euphorea to the puriest F1 fans, as well as keep the kids enterained with the long straights. The track and cars have been tested with all saftey regulations passed and ensured, all riders are given helmats and protective suits. For a takeaway memory you can purchase the Go Pro video to re-live this thrilling experience.
