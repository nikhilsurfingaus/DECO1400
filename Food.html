Activities > Food

Street Food

Street food is a staple of Excitement Islands diverse cuisine. The street food market offers food from around the world including Asia, the middle east, Africa, Central and South america as well as well as Western Europe. You will not be dissapointed spending hours on end fueling up your stomach in the street food market.


Food Trucks

Food trucks pop up around the island with a vairety of food. This inlcudes our dessert van from ice cream to fudge your sweet tooth will be satisfied. Whilst our Central American trucks sell food prices below $5 including fries, hot dogs, beagels, burgers, wings, ribs, poki salad just to name a few. Don't worry about dietry needs, food trucks vary from non-vegetarian, vegetarian, vegan and gluten free food on offer.


Resturants

Many Resturants are avaliable for our guests which vary from cheap to expensive to cure any food lovers taste pallete. Most of the Resturantsare based on the Marina bay, which offers a view of the dock and ocean to go along with your wine and delicious meal.
