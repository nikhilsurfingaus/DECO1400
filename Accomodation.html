Singles Room

Our Singles resort accommodation offers both comfort and luxury for our guests booking a single room. with this room you will have a queen size bed, follwed by a Danish styled bathroom and kitchen, finally our Italian styled living room. You will not be dissapointed by the ocan view from your private balcony.



Family Room

Our family resort rooms ensure that your family is satisfied and filled with fun when your not out exploring Excitement Island. The room inlcudes a queen bed for parents, as well as single beds for the kids. All rooms are fully serviced with a bathroom as well as living room including toys, gaming consoles and many more kids activities. Your family can also take a dip in you own private pool.



Couples Room

Let your relationship reach new heights with a romantic getaway in our Couples Room. Steal away into a One Bedroom private pool villa, where a tranquil glass-enclosed balcony is an ideal spot for intimate cocktails and cuddles overlooking the ocean. Or take the romance to the spa, with a dual Swedish massages to relax side-by-side as talented therapists soothe your cares away.



Friends Room

We also have a Freinds Room Avliable where up to 10 guests share a single floor, each room containing all the features of the single room. The floor provides friends with a patio, sea views, seating area, satellite flat-screen TV, kitchen, individual private bathroom with shower, table tennis equipment, XBOX ONE and PS5 consoles and finally a shared 25 x 10m private pool.
